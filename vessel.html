MS Delphin



         


  A smaller, yacht-like ship

The smaller of our ship, MS Delphin offers an informal and enjoyable way to cruise, effortlessly taking you to a host of amazing destinations.


With no more than 500 passengers on board, the ship is reserved exclusively for adults so the atmosphere is always refined, relaxed and sociable. 


There just plenty of entertainment and activities tailored for you, and despite being small, she still manages to pack in a surprising array of features.

 

Fine dining

Full waiter service or buffet? Open seating or the same table each meal time?


When it comes to dining on MS Delphin, it's all about choice – your choice – and there are restaurant offering different dining experiences.


Best of all, all meals are always included in the price, with no added supplements or charges.

Pamper yourself in the spa

We've teamed up with Decléor, the world's leading aromatherapy skincare brand, so you can enjoy the ultimate in relaxation on board.


Designed for pure pampering, choose from a varied menu of tempting treatments, all using premium essential oils and natural ingredients. This includes facials, massages, manicures, tailored wellbeing programmes and special treatments just for gentlemen.

Cabin grades

Once you're back on the ship after a busy day's sightseeing, there's nothing better than returning to your own private cabin to rest, relax and enjoy 24-hour room service.


MS DELPHIN may be a smaller ship, but that doesn't mean you have to compromise on your own personal space as the cabins are deceptively spacious with plenty of storage and all mod cons – and you're sure to be surprised just how light, bright and well designed the cabins are.


Offering a stylish nautical flair, there's a great choice of inside and outside cabins. Superior-grade rooms and cabins especially for solo travellers – these are limited, so they tend to sell out fast! Or if you really want to splash out, why not opt for a spacious Suite complete with butler service?
